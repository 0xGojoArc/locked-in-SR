###### Boolean
- `bool` keyword
- two possible values: `true` or `false`
- five operators: `!`, `==,` ` !=`, `&&`, `||`
- short circuit: `||` , `&&`
	- either one of the expression is enough to evaluate
- [[Security]]: examine Control flow, checks like access, pay attention to `||` Vs `&&` logics
